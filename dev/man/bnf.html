(Extended) Backus-Naur Form CombinedParser

Defining a EBNF parser can be done with the CombinedParsers.BNF.ebnf string macro. substitute is used to combine parts of the definition.

Warn

Left recursion is not yet supported (will lead to a stack overflow).
